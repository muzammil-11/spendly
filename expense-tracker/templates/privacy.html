Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is needed to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly expense tracking service — to display your records, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces; no personal data is sent as part of this request beyond a standard browser request. We do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. If this changes, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data we hold about you, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
